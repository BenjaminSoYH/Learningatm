Depression

This is a type of mental illness that repeatedly affects the thought process, emotions, and actions.

possible symptoms:

  • Loss of Motivation
  • Loss of interest
  • Insomnia
  • Overeating
  • Anxiety
  • Lack of Concentration
  • Suicidal Ideation
  • Extreme weight gain/loss

Anorexia

An eating disorder causing people to obsess about weight and what they eat.

possible symptoms:

  • dehydration
  • dizziness
  • Fainting
  • binge eating
  • impulsitivity
  • under weight
  • Suicidal Ideation
  • Extreme weight gain/loss
